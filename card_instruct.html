Instruction to install card(Apply for Koikatsu and SVS)

Step 1: In Explorer. Go to your game destinaton: "path to your game/UserData"

Step 2: Copy card to "chara" or "coordinate" folder. Card should be .png file and the card file is valid

For "chara" folder, there 2 folders name "male" and "female". If card is male, move to "male" folder and "female" for female card

You can watch this video for further information.(This apply for SVS, too)
